\emph{Olga Arsenievna Oleinik} (1925 - 2001) Soviet mathematician, best known for her work on partial differential equations.

She studied with Ivan Petrovsky at a university in Moscow, later she taught there.

A prolific author, Oleinik has \PMlinkname{Erd\H{o}s number}{ErdHosNumber} 3: With Vladimir Ilin and others, she co-authored a biographical sketch of Andrey Tikhonov in honor of his 80th birthday, while Ilin co-authored with Istv\'an Jo\'o a paper giving ``A uniform estimate of eigenfunctions and an upper estimate of the number of eigenvalues of the Sturm-Liouville operator'' in a Soviet journal. Jo\'o co-authored with Erd\H{o}s ``Some remarks on infinite series'' in a Hungarian journal.
