\emph{David Albert Huffman} (1925 - 1999) American mathematician, best known for Huffman coding used in many lossless data compression \PMlinkescapetext{schemes}. 

Huffman served in the U. S. Navy during World War II as a radar maintenance officer on a destroyer. After the war, he continued his studies at Ohio \PMlinkescapetext{State} University and earned a PhD from MIT in 1953 and joined the faculty there that year.
