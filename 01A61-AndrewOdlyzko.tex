\emph{Andrew Odlyzko} is a mathematician best known for disproving the Mertens conjecture with Herman J. J. te Riele. Odlyzko co-authored ``On the Density of Odd Integers of the Form $\displaystyle \frac{(p-1)}{2k}$ and Related Questions'' in {\it J. of Number Theory} 11 (1979) with Paul Erd\H{o}s, and thus has \PMlinkname{Erd\H{o}s number}{ErdHosNumber} 1. Odlyzko studied at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
